Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is Sitescape?

- Sitescape is a bookmarking website that allows you to save and organize your favorite websites and webpages in one place. It makes it easy to access your bookmarks from any device with an internet connection.

2. Is Sitescape free to use?

- Yes, Sitescape offers a free plan that provides essential bookmarking features. We also offer a premium subscription with additional features for those who want an enhanced experience.

3. How do I create an account on Sitescape?

- To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Follow the registration process, which will guide you through creating a username and password.

4. Can I access my bookmarks on multiple devices?

- Yes, your bookmarks are stored in the cloud, allowing you to access them from any device with an internet connection. Simply log in to your Sitescape account to see your saved bookmarks.

5. How do I add a bookmark to Sitescape?

- To add a bookmark, click the "Add Bookmark" or "Save Page" button, and then enter the URL and a name for the bookmark. You can also organize bookmarks into folders for better management.

6. Are my bookmarks private and secure?

- We take your privacy and security seriously. By default, your bookmarks are private. You can choose to share them with others if you wish. All data is encrypted to protect your information.

7. Can I import bookmarks from my web browser?

- Yes, Sitescape allows you to import your existing bookmarks from popular web browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, and Safari. Look for the import option in the settings.

8. What happens if I forget my password?

- If you forget your password, you can use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.
